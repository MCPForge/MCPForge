This is a custom Forge mod created to solve the chatgames on MCPrison.com. You will be able to use this mod securely as the MCPrison Staff does not do any screensharing on the network

FAQ

How do I use this?
Download and put it in the "mods" folder. Load up your forge and you can just click the chatgame and it will automatically solve it for you. YOU HAVE TO CLICK IT TO SOLVE IT!

Can I toggle this off?
Yes, just look in your keybinds

Do you support LabyMod?
Both the 1.8.9 and 1.12.2 version support LabyMod Forge

What's so special about 1.15.2
1.15.2 has a timer feature because this is the most updated version of stable Forge
